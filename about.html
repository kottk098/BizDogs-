BizDogs LLC isn't just a living space; it's an accelerator for first-year business students at the Labovitz School of Business and Economics. We combine residency with mentorship to forge the next generation of leaders.

Our Mission

To cultivate a high-impact living-learning community where academic rigor meets professional networking and lifelong social bonds.

BizDogs is dedicated to reducing the transitional friction for first-year students. By aligning living arrangements with the LSBE curriculum, we ensure that the lessons learned in the lecture hall are reinforced in the residence hall lounge.

A Legacy of Leadership

Tracing the path from a pilot program to a hallmark of the LSBE experience.

12

The Inception

BizDogs LLC was founded by LSBE faculty members who noticed a gap in social integration for business freshmen. The first cohort of 24 students pioneered the model.

17

Expanding Horizons

The program moved to Lake Superior Hall, doubling in size and adding the first dedicated faculty-in-residence office hours.

24

A Modern Hub

Today, BizDogs is a nationally recognized Living-Learning Community, serving as a model for holistic student development in business education.
