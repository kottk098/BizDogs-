Who Are We?

The BizDogs Living and Learning Community is a group of first-year, pre-business students within the Labovitz School of Business and Economics (LSBE), who elect to live together in a traditional residence hall community and take classes together, creating an environment where community members can thrive socially and academically.

By living together, students benefit from ease of finding others on the floor to participate in campus events or other social activities, study groups for LSBE core classes, and dovelop lasting friendships with students in the same community.
