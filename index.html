Who Are We?

Based in the heart of campus at **Lake Superior Hall**, BizDogs is more than just a dorm—it's a launchpad for your professional career. We bring together high-achieving first-year business students into a single floor, creating an environment where network building happens naturally.

By living together, students benefit from spontaneous brainstorming sessions, study groups for rigorous LSBE core classes, and direct access to faculty mentors who visit the community.
